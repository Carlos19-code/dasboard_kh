Introduction

KHUMOTONE is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' data. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

expand_more

We collect information you provide directly, such as name, email, and company when filling out contact forms. We also automatically collect data through cookies, such as your IP address, browser type, and visited pages, to enhance your experience.

How We Use Your Information

expand_more

Collected data is used for various purposes, such as improving our services, responding to your requests, personalizing your experience, and, with your consent, for marketing communications. We are committed not to use your information for purposes incompatible with those described here.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

expand_more

Our website uses cookies to optimize functionality and analyze traffic. You can manage cookie usage through your browser settings. Refusing cookies may affect the functionality of certain parts of the site.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

expand_more

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except when necessary to comply with legal obligations, protect our rights, or with service providers operating on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

expand_more

We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of Internet transmission is 100% secure.

User Rights

expand_more

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us through the channels provided in this policy.

Policy Updates
